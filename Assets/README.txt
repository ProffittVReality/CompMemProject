How to set-up experiment for new or returning Session:
1) Open the experiment scene at Assets/Scenes/SCENENAMEPLACEHOLDER
2) At the top of th Hierarchy view, select SESSION MANAGER
3) In the Inspector view under the SessionManager component, replace
   the Participant ID and other fields with the appropriate info.
   (if they are a returning participant, you only need to fill in ID.)
4) Press Play to begin the experiment. The system will automatically
   load in existing data if it is a returning participant.
5) To view Session info after the session, go to
   Assets/Resources/Sessions/, and select the appropriate ID.
